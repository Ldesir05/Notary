Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your appointment.

What identification do I need to provide?

You must present a valid, non-expired government-issued photo ID. This includes a Driver's License, Passport, or Military ID. The name on the ID must match the name on the document.

Can you notarize a document that is already signed?

No. Documents must be signed in the physical presence of the Notary. If you have already signed it, you may be asked to sign it again in my presence.

How much do you charge for travel?

Mobile fees vary based on distance and time of day. Standard travel starts at $25 within a 10-mile radius, plus the $10 state-mandated per-signature fee

Are you a lawyer?

I am not an attorney licensed to practice law and may not give legal advice or accept fees for legal advice.
